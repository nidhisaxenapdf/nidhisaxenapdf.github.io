Research : Publications

My research focuses on image processing based applications. I am a Post Doctoral Fellow at Computer Science & Engineering Department, IIT Roorkee where I work with Prof. R. Balasubramanian in the field of image processing and computer vision. I was recently awarded with Ph.D. in the topic of Image Processing (2014-2018). The title of my Ph.D. thesis is "New image fusion schemes using Hilbert vibration decomposition (HVD), fractional Fourier transform (FRFT) and graph signal processing (GSP) techniques" from Malaviya National Institute of Technology (MNIT), Jaipur under the guidance of Prof. K. K. Sharma. I did my Masters from MITS Gwalior in the Adhoc Networking (2008-2010), where I worked with Dr. Laxmi Shrivastava.
